About

Johnny is an emerging artist whose recent work explores themes of memory, place, and transformation. Working primarily in oil on canvas, he draws inspiration from daily life, the interplay of light and color, and the emotional resonance of ordinary objects.

His painting practice is informed by both observation and imagination, creating scenes that feel intimate yet universally accessible. Johnny is currently pursuing his studies in Studio Art and Data Analytics at Dickinson College.
